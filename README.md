# Attack Chains
Primary attacks in Tyrian Rain are not just a single attack - most melee primaries consist of an Attack Chain.
For example, the Warrior's Greatsword.
It starts out on Swing, then if an enemy is hit with Swing, it chains into Slice. If an enemy is hit with Slice, it chains into Brutal Strike. If that hits, it resets back to Swing.

# Survivor 1: The Warrior
A powerful warrior that becomes more dangerous the longer the battle rages on. Based on the Warrior profession from Guild Wars 2.

## Adrenaline and Burst Attacks
The Warrior builds up Adrenaline throughout a stage while dealing and taking damage. This is counted in Strikes of Adrenaline.
Every ten Strikes of Adrenaline is equal to one bar of Adrenaline. The maximum amount of Adrenaline is three bars -> 30 Strikes of Adrenaline.

Adrenaline is used to perform Burst Attacks. All Adrenaline is consumed to perform a single, powerful attack.
Each bar of Adrenaline affects the performed Burst Attack -> Lv.1 = 10 Strikes, Lv.2 = 20 Strikes, Lv.3 = 30 Strikes.

## Weapon Swap
The Warrior is a master-at-arms, and as such has access to multiple weapons. Weapons are selected in the Loadout screen, and affect the entire skill set of the Warrior.
Selectable weapons are: Greatsword, Hammer, Dual-wielded Axes, Dual-wielded Daggers, Mace and Shield, Sword and Torch, Longbow and a Rifle.

### Greatsword
The Greatsword is a jack-of-all trades weapon. It has decent reach, damage and speed.

[1,1] Swing: Attack Chain. Slash your foe for 150% damage. Chains into Slice.
[1,2] Slice: Attack Chain. Slice your foe for 200% damage. Chains into Swing.
[1,3] Brutal Strike: Attack Chain Ender. Hit your foe with a final brutal strike for 300% damage. Gain one Strike of Adrenaline. Resets Attack Chain.

[2] Bladetrail: Throw your greatsword at a foe so that it returns to you, dealing 250% damage and inflicting Cripple. Gain one Strike of Adrenaline.

[3] Whirlwind Attack: Whirl forward, slashing foes for 3x200% damage along your path. You are invulnerable during this skill. Gain two Strikes of Adrenaline.

[4] Hundred Blades: Root yourself in place, repeatedly striking foes in front of you for 8x150% damage, finishing with a final strike that deals 500% damage. Gain three Strikes of Adrenaline. 

[B] Arcing Slice: Burst Attack. Deliver a circular attack to foes around you, dealing 1000% damage and executing low-health foes. Gain Fury for 8/12/16 seconds. Gain 3/6/9 stacks of Might for 10 seconds.

### Hammer
The Hammer focuses on crowd-control - long-duration stuns and launches. It has decent reach, but deals lower damage and is slower than the Greatsword.

[1,1] Swing: Attack Chain. Bash your foe for 100% damage. Chains into Bash.
[1,2] Bash: Attack Chain. Bash your foe again for 150% damage. Chains into Smash.
[1,3] Smash: Attack Chain Ender. Smash the ground and damage nearby foes for 250% damage. Gain one Strike of Adrenaline. Resets Attack Chain.

[2] Fierce Blow: Weaken your foe with a fierce blow, dealing 300% damage and inflicting Weakness. Deal 100% increased damage to stunned targets. Gain one Strike of Adrenaline.

[3] Staggering Blow: Push back and stun nearby foes with a staggering blow, dealing 250% damage. Gain two Strikes of Adrenaline.

[4] Backbreaker: Stun your foe for a long time, dealing 400% damage. Weakened foes are stunned for longer. Recharge Fierce Blow on hit. Gain three Strikes of Adrenaline.

[B] Earthshaker: Burst Attack. Leap forward and slam your hammer down, dealing 700% damage around you and stunning nearby foes. Gain 3/6/9 stacks of Might for 10 seconds.

### Rifle
The Rifle is a ranged weapon with a focus on high single-target damage. Compared to the Longbow it is slower but more powerful, with less area-of-effect potential.

[1] Fierce Shot: Fire a shot at your target, dealing 250% damage. Gain one stack of Might for ten seconds and one Strike of Adrenaline on hitting a vulnerable target.

[2] Volley: Fire a volley of shots at your target, dealing 6x250% damage. Inflict one stack of Vulnerability per shot. Gain one Strike of Adrenaline.

[3] Brutal Shot: Leap backward while shooting your target for 300% damage, immobilizing them. Gain two Strikes of Adrenaline.

[4] Explosive Shell: Shoot an explosive round that explodes when it hits, dealing 800% damage in a small area. Inflict ten stacks of Vulnerability. Gain three Strikes of Adrenaline.

[B] Kill Shot: Burst Attack. Fire a powerful shot for 1000%/1500%/2000% damage. Gain 3/6/9 stacks of Might for 10 seconds.

## Traits
To spice up long-term runs, Tyrian Rain survivors gain Traits every five levels, up to level 15.

[Lv.5] Opportunist: Gain a Strike of Adrenaline upon stunning a foe. Gain Fury for 5 seconds upon stunning a foe.

[Lv.10] Might Makes Right: Heal 5% of maximum HP per stack of Might gained. Triggers per stack of Might gained. Gained Might is also given to nearby allies, at half the effect.

[Lv.15] Vengeance: Upon death, instead of dying, become invulnerable for 5 seconds. Upon killing your intended killer, heal 100% of maximum HP and gain 100% barrier. Cooldown of 120 seconds.

# Boons and Conditions
## Boons
Boons are powerful beneficent buffs.

Aegis: Completely absorb the next direct attack. Damage-over-time is unaffected.
Alacrity: Recharge skills 25% faster.
Fury: Critical chance and critical damage is increased by 20%.
Might: Gain 2% damage per stack. Max. 25 stacks.
Protection: Take 33% reduced direct damage. Damage-over-time is unaffected.
Quickness: Attack speed is increased by 20%.
Regeneration: Health regeneration is increased by 5 HP/s.
Resistance: Non-damage debuffs are ineffective/removed.
Resolution: Take 33% reduced damage-over-time. Direct damage is unaffected.
Stability: Resist all knockback.
Swiftness: Movement speed is increased by 33%.
Vigor: Skills classified as movement skills (mainly skills bound to Shift) recharge 66% faster.

## Conditions
Conditions are powerful debuffs.

Confused: Take 150% damage per stack on attack/skill usage. Max. 25 stacks.
Poisoned: Take 35% damage-over-time per stack. Healing effectiveness is reduced by 66%. Max. 25 stacks.
Tormented: Take 50% damage-over-time per stack while standing still. Take 150% damage-over-time per stack while moving. Max. 25 stacks.
Blinded: Miss the next direct attack.
Chilled: Movement speed is reduced by 66%. Skill recharge is 66% slower.
Crippled: Movement speed is reduced by 50%. Flying enemies are grounded.
Feared: Involuntarily flee from the inflictor.
Immobilized: Movement speed is reduced by 100%. Flying enemies are grounded.
Slowed: Attack speed is reduced by 50%.
Taunted: Chase after the inflictor until next attack lands.
Weakness: 50% of outgoing attacks become Glancing Blows - dealing 50% of otherwise dealt damage and unable to critically hit.
Vulnerability: Take 2% more damage per stack. Max. 25 stacks.
